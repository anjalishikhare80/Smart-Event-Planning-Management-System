Questions

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you get your pass.

01 What exactly is EventHub and how does it work?

EventHub centralizes the whole event lifecycle — creation, registration, engagement, and post-event analytics — in a single, intuitive workspace.

02 Who can benefit from using EventHub?

Event organizers, companies planning conferences, wedding coordinators, association admins, and anyone discovering events to attend. It scales from small meetups to large conferences.

03 How do I create and launch an event?

Sign up as an organizer, open your dashboard, choose "Create event," fill in the details, set your registration preferences, and publish. A step-by-step guide walks you through each stage.

04 Is EventHub free to use, or does it have pricing?

A generous free tier covers the core features most events need. Premium plans add advanced analytics and extra capacity as you grow.

05 Can I manage multiple events at once?

Yes — your dashboard lists every event you run, with registrations, engagement, and performance for each visible from a single screen.

06 How is my data protected on EventHub?

Encryption, regular security reviews, and role-based access keep your information and your participants' data protected at every step.
